Blackjack

  • Classic Blackjack

    The timeless version of the game that everyone knows and loves. Classic Blackjack is played with a standard 52-card deck and the objective is simple: beat the dealer by getting as close to 21 as possible without going over. With straightforward rules and a low house edge, this version is perfect for both beginners and seasoned players who enjoy the purest form of blackjack.

  • European Blackjack

    In European Blackjack, the dealer only receives one card face-up initially, and the second card is dealt after the player has made their decisions. This version typically uses two decks of cards, and there are stricter rules regarding doubling down and splitting. The game offers a unique twist on the traditional format, requiring a bit more strategy, making it a favorite among skilled players.

  • Vegas Strip Blackjack

    Inspired by the glamorous casinos of Las Vegas, Vegas Strip Blackjack is a popular variant played with four decks of cards. This version allows players to double down on any two cards and offers the option to re-split aces. With rules designed to enhance the excitement and strategic possibilities, Vegas Strip Blackjack is ideal for those who love fast-paced action with a classic feel.

  • Atlantic City Blackjack

    Named after the famous gambling hub, Atlantic City Blackjack is a variant played with eight decks of cards and offers several player-friendly rules. Players can double down after splitting and the dealer must stand on a soft 17, giving players a slight edge. The game also includes an option for late surrender, allowing players to recover half of their bet if they choose to surrender after the dealer checks for blackjack. This variant offers a balanced mix of strategy and fun.
